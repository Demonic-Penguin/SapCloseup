Starting Process: Enter a service order number to begin the close-up process.
Please enter a valid service order number (digits only).
Instructions

Enter the service order number to begin the close-up process. The system will:

  1. Verify the service order exists in SAP
  2. Open the ZIWBN transaction
  3. Retrieve all relevant service order information
  4. Guide you through the complete close-up workflow

Please ensure you have access to SAP before proceeding.
